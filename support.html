Frequently asked

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the paywall and tap Restore. Your premium access is tied to your Apple ID, so it comes back on any device signed in with the same account.

How do I cancel my subscription or free trial?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Quell → Cancel. If you're on a free trial, cancel at least 24 hours before it ends to avoid being charged.

Is my data private?

Yes. Quell has no account and no cloud — your progress, journal, voice notes and Ride clips stay on your device. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I turn the camera off?

The reaction-cam is off by default. You can toggle it any time in Settings inside the app. Your camera only ever runs during a Ride, and clips never leave your phone unless you share them.

What's free vs. premium?

Your counter, streak, journal, milestones, breathing, and a rescue a day (a Ride or a coach session) are free. Quell Plus unlocks unlimited rides and coaching for when the wave keeps coming back, plus your full recovery timeline and patterns. Every plan is shown up front, with no traps.

If you're struggling right now

Quell is a motivation tool, not a medical service. For health guidance about quitting, a free quitline can help: in the US, call 1-800-QUIT-NOW. If you are in crisis, call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or your local emergency number.
